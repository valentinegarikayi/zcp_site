CALL FOR SOLIDARITY

July 23, 2022

CALL FOR SOLIDARITY NATIONAL AND INTERNATIONAL 18th June 2022 in the case of Comrade Obert Masararure President of the Amalgamated Rural Teachers’ Union of Zimbabwe (ARTUZ), falsely arrested and accused of murder, as the teachers of Zimbabwe embark on job action due to salaries well below the Poverty Datum Line. Yesterday, 17th June 2022 Comrade Obert Masaraure, President of the Amalgamated Rural Teachers’ Union of Zimbabwe (ARTUZ) was refused bail at Harare Magistrate’s Court after being arrested for the murder of a friend.
